Reading tells you. Doing makes it stick.

You can read three chapters about how something works and still not know it. Build it — even briefly, even badly — and you'll remember it for years. Knowsy is the doing half of every subject we cover.

/ 02

Be wrong on purpose.

Every page is a safe place to try the wrong answer. Push the button you're not sure about. Run the thing that probably breaks. Knowsy just shows you what happened — no points off, no teacher watching. That's where understanding actually shows up.

Teach once. Stop answering it again.

For educators: stop redrawing the same diagram every semester. Hand students a working version of the lesson — they poke at it on their own time. You spend class on the questions that need a human, not the ones a simulator can answer.
